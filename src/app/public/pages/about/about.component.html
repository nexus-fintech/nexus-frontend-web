About Nexus Finance

Nexus Finance is a demonstration of a modern, distributed Fintech Architecture. It solves the challenge of decoupling transactional logic from analytical decision-making.

Built with high standards of engineering: Domain-Driven Design (DDD), Clean Architecture, and Microservices.
